Safeguarding Your Information

Data protection encompasses a range of practices, policies, and technologies designed to safeguard data from various threats, including unauthorized access, corruption, and loss. Effective data protection ensures that information remains accurate, secure, and available when needed. Key components of data protection include:


  • Data Backup
  • Compliance
  • Data Classification
  • Access Controls

Encryption/h3>

Encryption is a critical technology used to protect data by converting it from a readable format (plaintext) into an unreadable format (ciphertext). This process ensures that only authorized individuals with the appropriate decryption key can access the original information. Key aspects of encryption include:


  • Encryption Algorithms
  • AES (Advanced Encryption Standard)
  • RSA (Rivest–Shamir–Adleman)
  • Key Management
  • Protocols
